Alerts Overview: Displays recent alerts with status, source, and linked scoring records — for SOC analysts to see current pipeline state at a glance.

Needs Review Queue: Lists alerts routed as `needs_review` with model confidence and notes — for human reviewers and specialists to triage and resolve.

Auto‑Analyzed (High Confidence): Shows alerts auto‑marked `analyzed` with confidence and recommended actions — for on‑call analysts to quickly act on high‑confidence model outputs.

Scoring Results Summary: Aggregates `Scoring_Results` (counts, average confidence, severity breakdown) — for managers and data scientists to monitor model performance and workload.

Errors & Audit Log: Records failed runs, validation errors, and `error_reason` entries with record IDs — for engineers and auditors to debug failures and verify traceability.
